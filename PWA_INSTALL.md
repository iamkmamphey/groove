# Groove App - Installation Guide

## Progressive Web App (PWA) - Works on iPhone & Android âœ…

The easiest way to use Groove on your phone!

### For iPhone (Safari):
1. Open Safari on your iPhone
2. Navigate to: `http://10.0.0.39:8000/mobile-frontend.html`
3. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow)
4. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
5. Tap **"Add"** in the top right
6. The Groove app icon will appear on your home screen!

### For Android (Chrome):
1. Open Chrome on your Android phone
2. Navigate to: `http://10.0.0.39:8000/mobile-frontend.html`
3. Tap the **menu** (three dots)
4. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"** or **"Install app"**
5. Tap **"Install"**
6. The Groove app icon will appear on your home screen!
